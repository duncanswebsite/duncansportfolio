About Me

Hey, I’m Duncan Stewart. A graphic designer who thrives on structure but loves flipping it on its head. Clean lines, bold contrasts, and sharp, intentional design are my comfort zone, but I’m not afraid to push boundaries to make something stand out.

I’m constantly experimenting with new techniques to keep my work fresh. My vibe? Bold, minimal, structured, but never boring.

When I'm not designing? I’m probably deep into a video game with my two boys or turning random creative sparks into projects that push my skills even further. You want something that grabs attention and leaves a mark? Let’s make it happen.
